# Brief 02 — fusion, restless urgency to release

An energetic jazz-fusion instrumental in 7/8, roughly 16-24 bars, key open to
the composer's choice, tempo fast (around 150-170 BPM felt per eighth-note
grouping). Emotional arc: restless, unsettled urgency building across the
piece to a triumphant, released climax near the end.

Deliverables: a lead sheet (melody + chords), a bassline, a drum pattern, and
a finished multi-track file ready for a DAW. Small ensemble (lead, keys,
bass, drums) is fine.
