# 13 — Dinamika dan Dramaturgi (Level 13)

## Energy curve

| Section | Bars | Energy |
|---|---|---|
| Intro | 1-2 | 15% |
| A | 3-6 | 30% |
| A′ | 7-10 | 45% |
| B | 11-14 | 70% (bar 14 spikes to 85% at the dominant-pedal peak) |
| Climax | 15-18 | 100% |
| Coda | 19-20 | 40% (final hit), then near-0% |

Monotonic rise from Intro to Climax (matches brief's "urgency building...
to a triumphant... climax"), then a deliberate drop for the coda rather
than a tail-off — the release needs a clear top before the piece ends,
not a plateau.

## Parameters used (not just volume)

- **Register**: sax climbs from mid-register motif (A) to wide leaps
  reaching its highest notes at the climax (see `04-melody.abc` Tahap 9).
- **Density**: tracked in `06-arrangement.md` (sparse -> full).
- **Harmonic tension**: tracked in `03-harmony.md`'s tension-map column
  (stabil -> outside -> dominan puncak -> resolusi).
- **Rhythmic activity**: hi-hat opens from straight eighths (A/A′) to
  16ths (B) to full ride+crash (climax) — see `09-drums.json`.
- **Number of instruments active**: 2 (intro) -> 3 (A) -> 3 dense (A′) ->
  4 (B) -> 4 full (climax) -> 2 fading (coda).
- **Silence**: the melody's empty "4" group in A/A′ (space) versus the
  climax's near-continuous ensemble activity is itself a dynamic
  parameter — space disappears exactly as energy rises.
