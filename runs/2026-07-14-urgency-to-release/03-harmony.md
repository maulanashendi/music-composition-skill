# 03 — Peta Harmoni (Level 3)

## Tonal center & harmonic rhythm

Tonal center: **D** throughout (no key change), but the *mode* moves —
D Dorian (restless/unsettled, verse material) → brightened to a D
Lydian/major-ish color at the climax (triumphant/released), then settles.
Keeping the same root the whole piece means the "release" reads as a
transformation of the same identity, not an unrelated new key.

Harmonic rhythm: mostly **1 chord per bar** (7/8 bars are short — one
change per bar already reads as active), except bar 14 where the dominant
pushes twice as hard by staying put but intensifying its alteration
(no extra chord symbol change, intensity carried by melody/drums instead
— avoids over-loading harmonic rhythm right at the pivot).

## Teknik harmonik dipakai

- Modal interchange (bVII Cmaj7#11, bVI Bbmaj7#11) untuk warna "restless"
  tanpa meninggalkan D sebagai pusat.
- Chromatic mediant (bII Ebmaj7#11) sebagai outside material di A′ — lihat
  Level 4 tahap 8 untuk bagaimana ini direalisasikan di melodi.
- Tritone substitution (Ab7#9 menggantikan D7 di bar 12) untuk memperkuat
  rasa "menjauh" sebelum dominant run bar 13-14.
- Dominant pedal / secondary dominant build (G7#11 → A7alt) sebagai
  approach nyata menuju klimaks.
- Mode brightening (Dorian → Lydian/major, sama root) sebagai mekanisme
  "release" — bukan modulasi ke key lain.
