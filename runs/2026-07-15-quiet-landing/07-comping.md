# Level 7 — Comping dan Voicing

**Voicing:** rootless/spread Rhodes di register C4–E5; bass memegang root/pedal. Common-tone dan top-note E diprioritaskan menuju coda.

**Comping cells:** (1) held 1-bar pad; (2) delayed answer pada beat 3; (3) short syncopated stab di and-of-3.

| Ruang lead | Cell |
|---|---|
| Bar 10, 14, 18, 22 (lead mulai rest/menurun) | delayed answer |
| Bar 12, 16, 24, 32 (lead phrase selesai) | held pad lalu diam |
| Bar 27→28 (tension lead C#→E) | short stab di akhir bar 27, lalu silent sepanjang bar 28 agar resolve gitar terbaca |
| Bar 41–48 (E held) | held sparse voicing, density turun |

Comping tidak uniform: serangan 0–2 per bar menurut ruang lead.
