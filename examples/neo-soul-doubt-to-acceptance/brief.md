# Brief 01 — neo-soul, doubt to acceptance

A laid-back neo-soul/chill-jazz instrumental, roughly 16-24 bars, in C minor,
4/4, tempo around 78 BPM. Emotional arc: it should feel like quiet late-night
self-doubt at the start, and by the end resolve into a calm, unforced sense of
acceptance — not a big triumphant ending, just settling.

Deliverables: a lead sheet (melody + chords), a bassline, a drum pattern, and
a finished multi-track file ready for a DAW. Small ensemble (lead, keys or
rhodes, bass, drums) is fine.
